# Digoal Research 06: Timeline

## Scope

- As-of date: 2026-05-22.
- Focus: public profile milestones, career and community milestones, publication phases,
  and the most recent twelve months of visible activity.
- Source policy: prefer dated primary sources from `digoal/blog`, GitHub public
  profiles, and event/organization pages. Zhihu, WeChat public-account pages,
  Baidu Baike, and Baidu Zhidao were not used.
- Privacy boundary: this is a public timeline. It avoids family details from the
  retrospective source and avoids inferring private motives for career changes.

## Evidence Legend

| Mark | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `Primary` | Digoal's own dated writing, self-profile, GitHub profile, or the dated archive he maintains. |
| `Event primary` | Organizer, host, official community, or conference record for a public event/role. |

biographical claim made by a source.

## Findings

### Working Summary

- The public timeline is unusually well anchored by Digoal's own archive. His
  current `me/readme.md` gives month-level career phases from Qware through
  SkyMobi, Alibaba Cloud, and the April 2026 COSDA role; the dated blog archive
  supplies the publication chronology.
- Community identity appears early and stays durable. A 2011 PostgreSQL event
  record already lists `周正中 Digoal Zhou` as a speaker; a 2013 PostgreSQL wiki
  page uses his email for speaker submissions and lists two `digoal` talks;
  later self-records add the PostgreSQL China community, community university,
  PostgreSQL branch, ACED, advisory roles, and OSCAR award.
- The most visible recent shift is not a departure from databases but an
  expansion around them: AI papers, AI4DB/DB4AI, DuckDB/DuckLake, source-reading
  courses, AI coding/agent skills, finance, book notes, and cross-domain
  "思维筑基课" material all appear in the 2025-2026 archive alongside PostgreSQL
  previews and operations material.

### Timeline Interpretation For The Perspective Skill

- Digoal's durable public center of gravity is practical database work plus
  community transmission: database selection, operations, internals,
  architecture, talent cultivation, courses, and open-source ecosystem work.
- Career-role shifts line up with publication widening. The SkyMobi and early
  Alibaba period reads like a hands-on PostgreSQL/DBA/architecture archive;
  the later Alibaba open-source/product period adds ecosystem and method posts;
  the 2025-2026 cadence adds AI-assisted reading, skills, agents, finance, and
  cross-domain thinking without dropping PostgreSQL.
- The recent twelve-month record suggests an "industrialized learning and
  publishing" pattern: turn fresh technology or source trees into paper notes,
  lessons, commentary, and skill/course artifacts quickly. That is an archive
  inference, not a claim about the unseen production process.

### Date And Source Cautions

1. Do not flatten the first-PostgreSQL date. Public material says both 2008
   (`S2`) and 2010 (`S9`); use an approximate formulation unless a stronger
   contemporaneous record is found.
2. The self-profile contains a stale-looking sentence saying "曾就职于阿里云(至今)"
   while its career section ends the Alibaba phase at `2026.4` and starts COSDA
   at `2026.4`. Use the explicit month-level career sections for timeline work
   and avoid inventing a resignation day or reason.
3. "PostgreSQL China community founder" is a public self-description repeated
   across profile material. The 2011 event page corroborates early public
   participation, but it is not a legal founding document.
4. Blog file counts are archive counts from the local mirror, not audited
   cross-platform publication or readership counts. Migrated 163 Blog material
   makes pre-2010 and early-2010 volume especially incomplete.
5. The publication phase boundaries are analytic convenience. Treat them as
   `Inference`, not direct quotes from Digoal.
