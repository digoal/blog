# Role Checklist

Use this checklist when filling the seven intermediate role files.

## User

- User segment and urgent job.
- Current workaround or substitute.
- Setup, onboarding, and first-value path.
- Frequency, retention trigger, and switching cost.
- Trust, privacy, safety, reliability, and support blockers.
- Willingness to pay and budget owner.

## Investor

- Market category and demand driver.
- Growth rate or adoption proxy.
- Revenue model and margin signal.
- Defensibility: data, workflow, network, distribution, brand, regulation, ecosystem.
- Capital intensity and dependency risk.
- Comparable cases, exits, or failures.

## Product Manager

- Positioning and target user.
- Core workflow and activation event.
- Feature completeness versus user job.
- Roadmap bets and sequencing.
- Metrics: activation, retention, conversion, expansion, quality, cost-to-serve.
- Pricing-product fit.

## Market Operator

- Best acquisition channels.
- Funnel bottlenecks.
- Content and campaign hooks.
- Proof assets: demos, case studies, benchmarks, testimonials.
- Community, referral, affiliate, or ecosystem loops.
- Low-cost experiments and channel risk.

## Brand Operator

- Category narrative.
- Brand promise and proof points.
- Trust assets.
- Differentiation language.
- Tone and memorable assets.
- Reputation risks and crisis triggers.

## Competitor

- Direct competitors, indirect substitutes, and incumbents.
- Product vulnerabilities.
- Distribution vulnerabilities.
- Pricing and bundling attack paths.
- Feature parity risks.
- Likely response by strong incumbents.

## Partner

- Partner types and incentives.
- Integration, co-selling, marketplace, or reseller logic.
- Data/API/process requirements.
- Shared customer value.
- Partner conflict and dependency risk.
- Partnership priority and first outreach target.
